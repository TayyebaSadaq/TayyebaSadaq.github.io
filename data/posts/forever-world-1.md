## The vision

New years of 2026 came around quick after my graduation. Having recently landed a job as well I found most my days orienting around work and more learning. I played games here and there but nothing that was lasting...jumping from games to games I came across several videos on youtube about starting a forever world. By January 30th I made a decision to start one of my own, to see if I could commit and stop deleting worlds every month and have more fun with the game, do more than build a house and leave XD

## The mods

I'll be the first to admit that while minecraft is a nice cosy game to play, there are some mods and textures I can't live without since playing on PC. So in that vein I have a few mods that I can't play without and others that just make the game look nicer for myself (which in hand has kept me motivated to play)

Here's a little list of my top mods, shaders and resource packs that I've found made the game that much better:<br>
